Smart Traffic Management System

The Smart Traffic Management System uses real-time monitoring, intelligent algorithms and automated signal control to reduce congestion, improve road safety and provide faster movement for emergency vehicles.

Traffic Monitoring

Live camera based monitoring to detect congestion and traffic density.

Smart Signals

AI based signals adjust timing according to vehicle load.

Emergency Priority

Ambulances and fire trucks get automatic green signals.

Live Traffic Map

Real-time traffic visualization using digital maps.
